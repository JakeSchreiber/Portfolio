I spent the ten-year span prior to my enrollment at Prime Digital Academy working as a graphic designer in print media. The steady decline of print publications brought me to a crossroads where I needed to decide whether I wanted to continue on a path in the creative field. I made the decision to build on my experience as a designer by shifting my focus from print towards the digital realm. My goal is to find an agency that can help me continue to grow as a developer while utilizing my strengths as a designer.
